About Me

Intro

Hi, I am Jia. I am a graduate student at Lehigh University. My major is Industrial & Systems Engineering. I am a student fellow in the Visualizing Changes in Society project in the "Data 4 Impact" at Lehigh university. I applied data analysis, visualization, and web design to create a cognitive website to present the speed and direction of the societal shifts. I worked on this project last summer, and I will continue working on it in the fall 2020 semester.

Throughout my graduate study, I worked on multiple projects related to machine learning and supply chain management. Before my graduate study at Lehigh University, I graduated with a B.A. in Accounting from Donghua University, Shanghai, China. I have worked in KPMG Shanghai office as an audit associate for two years after graduating from Donghua University.

My concentration is in Optimization, Machine Learning and Database Management. As a goal oriented and fast forward team player, my academic and professional career is focused on applying advanced analytics and big data techniques to improve efficiency and create value in the world. I enjoy playing ping pong and film random stuff.

I will be graduating in Dec 2020 and I'm actively looking for full-time opportunities as a data analyst/data scientist.
